# Code Of Conduct

Be kind, direct, and useful.

Artshelf is a small technical project. Keep discussion focused on the work, assume
good intent, and make room for people with different backgrounds and experience
levels.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, doxxing, spam, or
deliberately disruptive conduct.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors who make
the project unsafe or unproductive.
